Cautions

You will use that input type when you want to enter your text by your own or by copying and pasting.

Steps:

1- In the choose your input type page, click on text then submit.

2- You will be taken to a page where there is a text area.

3- Add your content to the text area. (make sure your text doesn't have numbers only)

4- Choose your style and resolution.

5- Click submit.

Note: If you want to clear the content you added you can do that with just one click by clicking remove.

You will use that input type when you want to enter your text as an image or a screenshot

Steps:

1- In the choose your input type page, click on image then submit.

2- You will be taken to a page where there is an upload button.

3- Click on it and then upload your image.

4- Choose your style and resolution.

5- Click submit.

Note: If you want to remove the image you added and add a new one you can do that with just one click by clicking remove.

You will use that input type when you want to enter your text by specifying in which page does it exists in a PDF.

Steps:

1- In the choose your input type page, click on PDF then submit.

2- You will be taken to a page where there is an upload button.

3- Upload your PDF and enter your page number in the box that appears.

4- Choose your style and resolution.

5- Click submit.

Note: If you want to remove the PDF you added and add a new one you can do that with just one click by clicking remove.

Styles

In our website, you have 3 different kinds of generated pictures styles.

- Pixel Perfect: This is our main style, it is good when your page has a description of a location or a person.

- Kids: This style is good for children stories, especially when the characters are kids or animals.

- Novels: This style is good for novels and books that are most probably preferred by adults.

Resolution

You can also pick from 3 different kinds of resolutions.

- Landscape: It is good if your page has so many places.

- Square: It is good if your page has a mix of events.

- Portrait: It is good if your page is describing a character.

- Our target is not generating a detailed image of the page, but helping you imagine it.

- Your generated image fully depends on the page's content you pass as it goes through summarization and other AI models.

- If you want to generate pictures for many pages in your novel, you can pass each page of your novel individually, save the generated pictures and then merge them using a PDF editor website like www.ilovepdf.com

- You may find a bit differences if you are trying to generate a picture for a popular characters like Rapunzel, Superman, Tom & Jerry and other cartoons due to copyrights.
